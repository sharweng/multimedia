Rotoscoping Progress

Follow my journey through the art of rotoscoping from week 3 to week 13. Each video shows the progression of skills and techniques learned throughout the process.

The Rotoscoping Process

1

Video Reference Preparation

The first step involves selecting and preparing the video reference. This includes choosing the right footage, and adjusting frame rates.

2

Rotoscoping Each Frame

Next, individual frames are rotoscoped from the video. The background is replaced by solid green color for easier replacement of background later on.

3

Chroma Key

Removed the green background using chroma key.

4

Tricolor

Made the rotoscoped subject tricolor, which is light, medium, and dark shades. This is to easily apply the three shades of pencil later.

5

Pencil Effect

Applied multiple effects in AE so that the tricolored frame to replace the three shades of color from solid color to pencil.

6

Final Edits

The final step involves adding the original video for reference and adding my name and section.
